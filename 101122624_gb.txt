George Brown is a publically funded college in Canada, the college offers courses in: arts, business, information technology, design,
construction and engineering, health sciences, the culinary arts, etc. With three campuses in downtown Toronto courses are offered
in person, online or hybrid delivery. First opened in 1968 it has expanded ever since with it's latest campus opening this year 
the waterfront campus which will support medical studies and the school of design with features like a VR and AR lab,
and spaces for prototyping and innovation. With courses taught by and designed by industry leaders not matter what you are
interested in you can expect a first rate education that will give you the skills you need to succeed.
