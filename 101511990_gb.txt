George Brown College – Toronto, Ontario

George Brown College is a public college of applied arts and technology located in downtown Toronto, Canada. Established in 1967, it offers more than 160 career-focused programs, including diplomas, degrees, and postgraduate certificates. The college has three main campuses — Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront — all close to major industry hubs.

George Brown emphasizes practical, hands-on learning through co-ops, labs, and field placements, helping students gain real-world experience. It has strong connections with Toronto’s business, health, and technology sectors, giving graduates better employment opportunities. With a diverse international student community and modern facilities, George Brown College is known for providing high-quality, industry-relevant education in the heart of Canada’s largest city.
